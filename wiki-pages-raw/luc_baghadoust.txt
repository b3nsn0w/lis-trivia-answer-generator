'''Luc Baghadoust''' is a Producer working on ''[[Life is Strange (franchise)|Life is Strange]]'' and Life is Strange 2 at [[Don't Nod|DONTNOD Entertainment]] since 2013.

== Trivia ==
* Luc grew up in Maurepas and currently lives in Saint-Cloud, France.
* Luc is married to his wife Jessica since July 2, 2011.
* Luc and his wife are vegetarians. The fact that Luc is vegetarian motivated Dontnod to include the option to choose a vegetarian meal, Belgian waffles, in the [[Two Whales Diner]] scene in [[Episode 2: Out of Time|Episode 2]] of ''Life is Strange''.<ref>[[Kinda Funny Stage Show E3 2015]] (June 19, 2015)</ref>
* He has an older sister named Sarah.
* He likes heavy metal and plays bass in his own band, ''KAIZEN''.
* Luc is a member of the biggest ''Life is Strange'' [https://www.facebook.com/groups/1578433839080885/?fref=ts fan group on Facebook].
