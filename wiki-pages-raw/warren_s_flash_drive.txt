== Episode One - "[[Episode 1: Chrysalis|Chrysalis]]" ==
[[File:Dana-flashdrivenote.png|thumb|left|120px]] Shortly after exiting the [[Blackwell Academy]] building, Max receives a [[Max's Messages|message]] from Warren asking for his flash drive to be returned. She heads to [[Max's Room|her dorm room]] and finds out that [[Dana Ward]] has borrowed the drive to entertain herself while studying. Max then has to sort out the drama between [[Juliet Watson]] and Dana to finally get to [[Dana's Room|Dana's room]] and grab the flash drive. She can also plug it into [[Dana's Computer|Dana's computer]] (or [[Max's Computer|her own]]) to check its contents. After returning the drive to Warren on the [[Blackwell Parking Lot]], Max and Warren talk about the various films contained on it.

== Trivia ==
*After grabbing the flash, Max says: "Must protect my precious, so Max never has to chase it down again." This is a [[References (Life is Strange)|reference]] to "[[w:c:lotr|''The Lord of the Rings'']]".
*Warren's flash drive has colored sides and looks like three cubes connected together. This might be a reference to the ''[https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Rubik's_Cube Rubik's Cube].''
*[[File:ComputerScreen Chloe-flashdrive.png|thumb|100px]] During the investigation in [[Chloe's Room|Chloe's room]] in "[[Episode 4: Dark Room|Dark Room]]," the background screen on [[Chloe's Computer|Chloe's laptop]] is the screen previously seen in Episode 1 that shows the contents of Warren's flash drive; however, it is now minus the MAX folder and has a green border. This appears to be a reused and modified asset.
