= Cissy Jones =

[[File:Cissy-Jones.jpg|thumb]]
'''Cissy Jones''' is the voice actress for [[Joyce Price]], the [[Homeless Lady]] and [[Diner Woman|one unnamed minor character]] in ''[[Life is Strange]]''. She’s an up-and-comer with credits in many well-known games, playing the likes of Doctor Duff/Doctor Patricia in <em>Fallout 4</em> and more characters than any actor in Telltale’s <em>The Walking Dead</em> (such as Michonne). Most recently, she voiced Delilah, one of the two leads in <em>Firewatch</em>. Her official website can be found [http://www.cissyjones.com here].
