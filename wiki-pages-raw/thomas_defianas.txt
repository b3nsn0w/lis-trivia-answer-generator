'''Thomas Defianas''' (born August 31, 1990) is an enrivonment artist who has been working for [[Don't Nod]] since 2014. He's worked on all ''[[Life is Strange (franchise)|Life is Strange]]'' related installments directed by Dontnod, including the original ''[[Life is Strange]]'', ''[[The Awesome Adventures of Captain Spirit]]'' and ''[[Life is Strange 2]]'', as well as Dontnod's ''Vampyr''.
