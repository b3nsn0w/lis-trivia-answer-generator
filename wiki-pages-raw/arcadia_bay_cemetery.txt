== Life is Strange ==
The cemetery appears in the last cutscene of the game if [[Maxine Caulfield|Max Caulfield]] chooses to sacrifice [[Chloe Price]]. The location is not explorable.

Chloe's funeral takes place here, and is well-attended by Max, [[Joyce Price]], [[David Madsen]], [[Principal Wells]], [[Frank Bowers]] and his dog [[Pompidou]] and various Blackwell students including [[Warren Graham]], [[Kate Marsh]], [[Victoria Chase]], [[Justin Williams]], [[Trevor]] and [[Dana Ward]]. Chloe's grave is located next to that of her father [[William Price|William]].

The [[Blue Butterfly|blue butterfly]] appears in the last moments of the game on Chloe's coffin.

== Before the Storm ==
In the ending of the bonus episode "[[Farewell]]", [[Chloe Price (Before the Storm)|Chloe]] and [[Joyce Price (Before the Storm)|Joyce]] are seen attending [[William Price (Before the Storm)|William Price]]'s funeral. They are accompanied by [[Maxine Caulfield (Before the Storm)|Max]] for a short time, before the latter leaves with her parents for [[Seattle]].
