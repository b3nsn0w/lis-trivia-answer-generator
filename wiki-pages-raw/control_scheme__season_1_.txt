[[File:Controls (PC).png|thumb|243x243px|How I Move: The controls menu for the PC version of ''Life is Strange''.]]
The '''Control Scheme''' page lists the default control keys for ''[[Life is Strange]]'' across six platforms: PC, Mac, PlayStation 3/4 and Xbox 360/One.

==PC==
<blockquote>[[Image:Button-pc-mouse.png|thumb|left|50px]] Drag the mouse to aim at a object/person and hold Left Mouse Button to interact with it. Then, drag the mouse towards the interaction and release Left Mouse Button to look at the photo.</blockquote>

== Mobile (iOS/Android) ==
The controls have been simplified for touch screens on mobile. On Android, controller support is available.

{| class="wikitable"
!Symbol
!Name
!Primary Function
|-
|<center>[[File:button-mobile-journal.png|50px|link=]]</center>
|Journal Menu
|To access or exit Max's journal, tap the journal icon in the top right corner.
|-
|<center>[[File:button-mobile-leftscreen.png|50px|link=]]</center>
|Movement
|To move, tap anywhere on the screen or swipe your thumb on the '''left side''' of the screen.
|-
|<center>[[File:button-mobile-rightscreen.png|50px|link=]]</center>
|Camera
|To look around, swipe your thumb on the '''right side''' of the screen.
|-
|<center>[[File:button-mobile-rewind.png|50px|link=]]</center>
|Rewind
|To rewind time, tap on the spiral icon in the top left corner of the screen and then tap on the fast-rewind or rewind icon.
|-
|<center>[[File:button-mobile-undo.png|50px|link=]]</center>
|Undo
|Step back in time to your last important action, represented by a notch on the spiral.
|-
|<center>[[File:button-mobile-skip.png|50px|link=]]</center>
|Skip
|Tap the bottom right corner of the screen to skip a previously viewed scene.
|-
|<center>[[File:button-mobile-interacticons.png|50px|link=]]</center>
|Look/Interact
|To interact with objects and people, tap on an object and then select an action.
|}
