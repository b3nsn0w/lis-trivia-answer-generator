= Michelle Barenchi =

[[File:SwimLikeAnOtter-flyer.png|thumb|120px]]
'''Michelle Barenchi''' is a retired Physical Education teacher employed at [[Blackwell Academy]] as the coach for the [[The Otters]]. A flyer advertising her services can be seen on a noticeboard in the [[Blackwell Academy Hallway|Blackwell Hallway]] in [[Episode 1: Chrysalis|Episode 1]], [[Episode 2: Out of Time|Episode 2]], and [[Episode 5: Polarized|Episode 5]]. It reads as follows:

{{Bigquote
|Swim Like An Otter!

Experienced, retired PE teacher and former Otter Champion will personally train you to glide through the water and embrace your inner otter! For details, contact Michelle Barenchi at WaterLogged@ArcadiaBayGym.wet
}}

== Trivia ==
* There is another staff member at Blackwell Academy with the same last name, [[An-Marie Barenchi]]. She is the school nurse and there is a possibility that she is related to Michelle Barenchi.
