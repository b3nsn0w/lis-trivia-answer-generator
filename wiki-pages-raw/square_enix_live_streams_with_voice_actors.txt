= Square Enix Live Streams with Voice Actors =

{{Stub}}[[File:Squarestream-ep1.jpg|thumb|Announcement image for the first livestream.]]
Square Enix hosts a '''livestreams''' series of voice actors playing the original ''[[Life is Strange]]''. The streams were hosted by Eric So in the past, the North American Square Enix Social Media Manager (Senior Manager, Interactive Marketing).<ref>[https://www.linkedin.com/in/ericso LinkedIn page for Eric So]</ref>

== Episode One - "[[Episode 1: Chrysalis|Chrysalis]]" ==
[[File:Life Is Strange Stream w Hannah Telle & Nik Shriner|thumb|center|335 px]]
On July 27th, 2016, Square Enix hosted their second official livestream on Twitch.tv with special guests [[Hannah Telle]] ([[Maxine Caulfield|Max Caulfield]]) and [[Nik Shriner]] ([[Nathan Prescott]]).

The stream was set up to celebrate [[Episode 1: Chrysalis]] going free on all platforms. Fans had the opportunity to ask the special guests questions through the Twitch chat as well as win four vinyl soundtracks and six Steam Codes for Life is Strange.<ref>[http://na.square-enix.com/us/blog/life-strange-live-stream-hannah-telle-nik-shriner Square Enix Blog post about Episode 1 stream]</ref>

: ''(Click [https://www.twitch.tv/squareenix/v/80330370 here] to watch the Twitch VOD.)''

== Episode Two - "[[Episode 2: Out of Time|Out of Time]]" ==
[[File:VOD Life Is Strange - Ep 2 w Hannah Telle (Max), Dayeanne Hutton (Kate) & Nik Shriner (Nathan)|thumb|center|335 px]]
On September 28, 2016, Square Enix hosted a similar livestream of [[Episode 2: Out of Time]]. [[Dayeanne Hutton]] ([[Kate Marsh]]) was invited to join a returning Hannah Telle and Nik Shriner. The fans could once again win a vinyl soundtrack as well as [[Chloe Price]]'s tri-bullet necklace as a special reward.<ref>[http://na.square-enix.com/us/blog/life-strange-ep-2-live-stream-voice-actors Blog post about Episode 2 stream]</ref>

: ''(Click [https://www.twitch.tv/squareenix/v/91898843 here] to watch the Twitch VOD.)''

== Episode Three - "[[Episode 3: Chaos Theory|Chaos Theory]]" ==
[[File:Life Is Strange - Ep 3 w Hannah Telle (Max), Dayeanne Hutton (Kate) & Nik Shriner (Nathan)|thumb|center|335 px]]
On November 9, 2017, Square Enix hosted a long-overdue Live Stream of [[Episode 3: Chaos Theory]].<ref group="note">This live stream was overdue due to the SAG-AFTRA voice actor strike having prevented it from taking place.</ref> Hannah Telle, Dayeanne Hutton, and Nik Shriner all returned to play this episode together. The fans could win an exclusive ''[[Life is Strange: Before the Storm]]'' computer keyboard and mouse designed by Corsair as well as three prizes of a [[Hawt Dawg Man]] mug with Chloe's tri-bullet necklace.

: ''(Click [https://www.twitch.tv/videos/188963138 here] to watch the Twitch VOD.)''

== Episode Four - "[[Episode 4: Dark Room|Dark Room]]" ==
On April 24, 2018, Square Enix hosted a livestream of [[Episode 4: Dark Room]] with Hannah Telle, Dayeanne Hutton, Nik Shriner and Cissy Johnes ([[Joyce Price]]) playing the game.

: ''(Click [https://www.twitch.tv/videos/254059192 here] to watch the Twitch VOD.)''
