}}The '''Water Tower''' is a non-explorable location in [[Arcadia Bay]]. It is located near the [[Train Tracks|train tracks]] in the [[American Rust Junkyard]].

== Appearance ==
The water tower is a tall, heavily rusted structure with the words "City of Arcadia Bay" printed in fading letters across its water tank. Blue graffiti is written on the water tank's side, reading "Not a single fuck was given that day".

== Episode Two - "[[Episode 2: Out of Time|Out of Time]]" ==
As [[Maxine Caulfield|Max Caulfield]] and [[Chloe Price]] traverse down the train tracks at the junkyard, the water tower can be seen overlooking the rails.
