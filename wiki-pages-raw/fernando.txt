'''Fernando''' is one of [[Maxine Caulfield|Max Caulfield]]'s friends in [[Seattle]]. He went to the {{Wiki|Fremont Troll}} with Max and [[Kristen]].

He can first be seen and mentioned in [[Episode 1: Chrysalis|Episode 1]] if Max looks at some photos in her [[Max's Room|room]]. She mentions that [[Kristen]] and Fernando were drunk at the Fremont Troll.
