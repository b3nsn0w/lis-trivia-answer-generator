= Auntie Marsh =

'''Auntie Marsh''' is [[Richard Marsh]]'s younger sister and [[Kate Marsh]]'s paternal aunt. In [[Episode 2: Out of Time|Episode 2]], "Out of Time", she sent Kate a letter, in which she shamed Kate's actions in the viral video.

[[File:Auntiemarsh-letter.png|thumb|Letter from Auntie Marsh to Kate]]

{{Bigquote
|Dear Katie

No spirit or devil could have prepared me for what you have done in that videotape.<br />
And like a jezebel you released your harlot image before God's eye.<br />
I will pray with my church for your soul to be saved from Eternal Hellfire.<br />

Auntie Marsh
}}
