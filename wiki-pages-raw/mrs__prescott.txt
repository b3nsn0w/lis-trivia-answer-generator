= Mrs. Prescott =

'''Mrs. Prescott''' is the wife of [[Sean Prescott]] and the mother of [[Nathan Prescott]] and [[Kristine Prescott]]. Her appearance and personality is unknown due to her not making an appearance in ''[[Life is Strange]]''. She is only mentioned in an [[Kristine Prescott#Kristine's E-Mail|email]] from Kristine to Nathan and a [[Media:Dr._Jacoby%27s_Letter_to_Nathan%27s_Father.jpg|letter]] from [[Dr. Jacoby|Nathan's psychiatrist]] which can be found in the [[Episode 4: Dark Room (Location)|Dark Room]] in [[Episode 4: Dark Room|Episode 4]].

== Trivia ==
* Her first name, Caroline, is revealed in [[Episode 1: Awake|Episode 1]] of ''[[Life is Strange: Before the Storm]]'' on a computer in the [[Drama Lab]]. She has contributed in some way (most likely monetarily) to the school play ''[[The Tempest]]''.
