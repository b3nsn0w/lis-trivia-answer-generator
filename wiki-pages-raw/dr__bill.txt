= Dr. Bill =

'''Dr. Bill''' is a syndicated radio host and a book author in ''[[Life is Strange]]'' and his books can be discovered throughout the game. He is first mentioned in "[[Episode 1: Chrysalis|Chrysalis]]" when [[Maxine Caulfield|Max Caulfield]] finds a maternity book written by him in [[Dana Ward]]'s [[Dana's Room|room]]. In the [[Alternative Timeline|alternative timeline]], there are books about being a good father and mother, found in [[Joyce Price]]'s and [[William Price]]'s [[Joyce and David's Room#Alternate_Timeline|bedroom]]. [[Chloe Price]] also compares Max to Dr. Bill when Max asks her how her parents are doing. [[Sean Prescott]] suggests contacting Dr. Bill in order to deal with his son [[Nathan Prescott|Nathan]]'s mental problems. A book about rage control can be found in [[Nathan's Room|Nathan's room]].
