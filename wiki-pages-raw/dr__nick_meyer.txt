= Dr. Nick Meyer =

[[File:Nick Meyer Book.png|thumb|90px]]
'''Dr. Nick Meyer''' is a scientist and published author. He has published a book on [[Time Travel|time travel]], borrowed from the library by [[Maxine Caulfield|Max Caulfield]] at the recommendation of [[Warren Graham]] when she is researching the subject. The book can be seen on Max's desk at the beginning of [[Episode 2: Out of Time|Episode 2]].

==Trivia ==
* The name Nick Meyer is probably a [[References (Life is Strange)|reference]] to {{Wiki|Nicholas Meyer|Nicholas "Nick" Meyer}}, an American screenwriter and movie director. He is mainly known for having directed the 1979 time travel movie'' {{Wiki|Time_After_Time_(1979_film)|Time After Time}}''.
