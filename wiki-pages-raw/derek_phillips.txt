= Derek Phillips =

{{Stub}}[[File:Derek_Phillips.png|200px|right|Derek Phillips]]
'''Derek Phillips''' (born April 18, 1976 in Miami, Florida, United States) is the voice actor for [[Mark Jefferson]], [[Samuel Taylor]], [[Zachary Riggins]] and [[Cast (Life is Strange)#Derek Phillips|three minor characters]] in ''[[Life is Strange]]''.

== Trivia ==
* The humor site ''cracked.com'' stated that the "Too obvious?" after Jefferson's revealing words from his lecture in [[Episode 1: Chrysalis|Episode 1]] was a note on the script that Derek accidentally read out loud.<ref>[http://www.cracked.com/article_23370_6-incredible-spoilers-hidden-in-famous-video-games_p2.html "6 Incredible Spoilers Hidden In Famous Video Games"]</ref> This should be interpreted as humor as it is not otherwise confirmed.
* [[Hannah Telle]] (voice actor for [[Maxine Caulfield|Max Caulfield]]) says that the recording session with Derek for the [[Dark Room (Location)|Dark Room]] lines in "[[Episode 5: Polarized|Polarized]]" (when Max is tied up on the floor) was "so creepy".<ref>Said during [[Dayeanne Hutton]]'s [https://www.twitch.tv/videos/163670230 Twitch live stream] on August 1, 2017.</ref>
