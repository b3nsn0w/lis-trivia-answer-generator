'''Pan Estates''' is a current project of the [[Prescott Family|Prescott family]] in [[Arcadia Bay]].

[[Joyce Price]] works at Pan Estates in the[[Alternative Timeline (Life is Strange)| alternative timeline]]. [[David Madsen]] has a confidential security plan of Pan Estates in [[David's Garage|his garage]].
==Trivia==
*The title Pan Estates, located in Arcadia Bay, is likely a reference to the Greek god Pan, whose home was the wilderness of the Greek Utopia called Arcadia. Pan was the god of nature and the wild.
