'''Dick Weber''' is a fisherman and resident of [[Arcadia Bay]]. A picture of him and a prize catch can be found on the "[[Fishing Wall Of Fame]]" within the [[Two Whales Diner]], alongside [[Andy Berry]] and [[Rich Conrad]].
