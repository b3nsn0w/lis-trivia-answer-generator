= Naoya Nosaka =

[[File:Naoya_Nosaka.jpg|thumb]]
'''Naoya Nosaka''' ('''野坂 尚也''') is the Japanese voice actor of [[David Madsen]] ('''デイビッド・マドセン''') for the [[Japanese Release (Life is Strange)|Japanese localization]] of ''[[Life is Strange]]''.
