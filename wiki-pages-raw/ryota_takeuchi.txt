= Ryota Takeuchi =

[[File:Ryota_Takeuchi.jpg|thumb]]
'''Ryota Takeuchi''' ('''たけうち りょうた''') / ('''竹内 良太''') is the Japanese voice actor of [[Frank Bowers]] ('''フランク・バワーズ''') for the [[Japanese Release (Life is Strange)|Japanese localization]] of ''[[Life is Strange]] ''and ''[[Life is Strange: Before the Storm]]''.
