= Dani Knights =

[[File:Dani_Knights.png|280px|right]]

'''Dani Knights''' (born November 26, 1988) is a South African actress born in Cape Town, South Africa. She is the voice actress for [[Victoria Chase]], [[Dana Ward]], [[Brooke Scott]], and [[Dr. Hedorah|one minor character]] in ''[[Life is Strange]]''.

== Career ==
Knights is a practiced singer and songwriter perusing an acting career in Los Angeles, California. She was a finalist in ''X-Factor''. Her first feature was in the 2014 film, ''Sins of Our Youth'' with Lucas Till. In the same year, she appeared in the short film ''Hardsell'' and the TNT television series ''Major Crimes''.

== Trivia ==
* Dani Knights also provides the automated voice for Max's voicemail, heard when listening to Nathan's message in episode five, "[[Episode 5: Polarized|Polarized]]" (uncredited for it).
