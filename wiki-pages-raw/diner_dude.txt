The '''"Diner Dude"''' is a resident of [[Arcadia Bay]]. He is sitting at the counter in the [[Two Whales Diner]].

== Episode Two - "[[Episode 2: Out of Time|Out of Time]]" ==
When [[Maxine Caulfield|Max Caulfield]] tries to speak with him, he says, "What do you want? I feel like shit. Too much beer. So spare me, please. Just let me suffer quietly," and "Leave me alone or I will vomit on you."

== Episode Five - "[[Episode 5: Polarized|Polarized]]" ==
He appears in the diner sequence of [[Max's Nightmare|Max's nightmare]], sitting at the counter at the same place as in "Out of Time".
