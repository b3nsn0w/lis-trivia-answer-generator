= Drive-In =

The '''Drive-In''' theater in Newberg, OR is the [[Locations (Life is Strange)|location]] to where [[Warren Graham]] invites [[Maxine Caulfield|Max Caulfield]] in "[[Episode 2: Out of Time|Out of Time]]" to watch the 70's ''Planet of the Apes'' marathon with him.
