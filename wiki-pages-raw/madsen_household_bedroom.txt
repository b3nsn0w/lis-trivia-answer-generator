'''Joyce and David's Room''' is located upstairs above the kitchen in the [[Madsen Household]]. It was the bedroom of [[Joyce Price]] and [[William Price]], before she started living with [[David Madsen]]. The room is equipped with a double bed, a nightstand, a cabinet and a wall closet. The window is located at the opposite side of the door.

== Episode One - "[[Episode 1: Chrysalis|Chrysalis]]" ==
When [[Maxine Caulfield|Max Caulfield]] enters the room, she has the chance to save the [[Blue Jay]] from death by the window using her [[rewind]] ability. It will then end up on top of the closet and will make additional appearances at the path leading to the [[lighthouse]] and in later episodes.

== Episode Three - "[[Episode 3: Chaos Theory|Chaos Theory]]" ==
Max may only look at the door into the room from outside as she is not sure if David is inside and she doesn't want to disturb David.

== Episode Four - "[[Episode 4: Dark Room|Dark Room]]" ==
Max may only look at the door into the room from outside.

== Alternative Timeline ==
In the [[Alternative Timeline|alternative timeline]], Max can talk to Joyce about Chloe, William and Joyce herself, who is sitting on the bed and reading a book.
