= Rue Altimore =

'''Rue Altimore''' is a French/Italian restaurant in [[Arcadia Bay]] that is mentioned in ''[[Life is Strange]]''. According to [[Maxine Caulfield|Max Caulfield]], it is the "most expensive restaurant in town".

== Episode Four - "[[Episode 4: Dark Room|Dark Room]]" ==
[[File:altimore-bill.png|x120px|left]]
If Max sided with [[David Madsen]] during the confrontation in [[Episode 3: Chaos Theory|Episode 3]], she will be able to find a bill for over $260 from the Rue Altimore restaurant, listing "Six-Course Couple's Dinner w/ Endless Wine" as the main order. The bill can be found lying near the telephone in the entryway of the [[Madsen Household]]. It is also apparent from a sticky note on the fridge that David took [[Joyce Price]] to the cinema after dinner.

== Trivia ==
*"Rue Altimore" translates from French as "Altimore street".
*One of the staff members is called Oswald. C., as evident from David's bill, who served them.
