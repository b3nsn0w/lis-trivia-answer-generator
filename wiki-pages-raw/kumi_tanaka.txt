= Kumi Tanaka =

[[File:Kumi Tanaka as Max Caulfield.jpg|thumb|220x220px|right]]
'''Kumi Tanaka''' ('''たなか久美''') / ('''田中 久美''') (born March 21, 1981) is the Japanese voice actress of [[Maxine Caulfield|Max Caulfield]] ('''マックス・コールフィールド''') for the [[Japanese Release (Life is Strange)|Japanese localization]] of ''[[Life is Strange]] ''and'' [[Life is Strange: Before the Storm]]''.
