= Commissioned Artwork =

[[File:Nathan_Dollar.png|thumb]]
This article contains '''artworks''' that were '''commissioned''' by publisher Square Enix.

== History ==
On June 3, 2015, Square Enix launched a series of 10 commissioned artworks titled "Friends For Life" by artist [[Emma Vieceli]].<ref>[http://emmavieceli.com/gallery/character-illustration/20792706 Gallery emmavieceli.com]</ref> It was associated with the hashtag #FriendsForLife. The series concluded on November 10, 2015,<ref>[https://twitter.com/LifeIsStrange/status/672113129862488064 Official Twitter post]</ref> but two additional artworks were later added.[citation needed]

On November 15, 2017, Square Enix launched a series of commissioned 8 "promotional artworks" titled "The One" by artist Elisa Romboli (Afterlaughs), who has previously been a fan of the game and created various [[Fan Art|fan arts]].<ref>[https://www.artstation.com/artwork/3OnlA Life is Strange - Before the Storm "The One" Promotional Artworks - Afterlaughs' ArtStation]</ref><ref>[http://lifeisstrange-blog.tumblr.com/post/167523197260/working-with-afterlaughs "Working with Afterlaughs", official Tumblr post]</ref> It was associated with the hashtag #TheOne, representing someone "who changed your life in such a meaningful way that their very presence defines that time of your life". The series concluded on May 8, 2018.<ref>[http://lifeisstrange-blog.tumblr.com/post/173705189710/this-final-commissioned-artwork-by-the-talented "Life is Strange â€” This final commissioned artwork by the talented...", official Tumblr post]</ref>

London-based artist [[Jan-Wah Li]] has been commissioned regularly for social media posts related to special occasions such as holidays or characters' birthdays.
