= Yasuhiro Mamiya =

[[File:Yasuhiro_Mamiya.png|thumb]]

'''Yasuhiro Mamiya''' ('''マミヤ ヤスヒロ''') / ('''間宮 康弘''') is the Japanese voice actor of [[Nathan Prescott]] ('''ネイサン・プレスコット''') for the [[Japanese Release (Life is Strange)|Japanese localization]] of ''[[Life is Strange]] ''and ''[[Life is Strange: Before the Storm]]''.
