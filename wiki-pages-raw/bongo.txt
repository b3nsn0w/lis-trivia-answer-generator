|appearance = [[Episode 1: Chrysalis]] <small>(photos)</small>}}'''Bongo''' is [[Chloe Price]]'s deceased cat. He was a gift from her parents, [[William Price|William]] and [[Joyce Price|Joyce]], and lived from 1999 to 2008. He was always part of Chloe and her friend [[Maxine Caulfield|Max Caulfield]]'s childhood adventures and superhero stories. After he got killed by a car, Chloe and Max made a gravestone for him located in Chloe's garden.

== Before the Storm ==
* Bongo can be mentioned in ''[[Life is Strange: Before the Storm]]'', in [[Episode 1: Awake|Episode 1]] during the [[Two Truths and a Lie]] game.
* In "[[Farewell]]", depending on the player's choices, Chloe may draw a picture of Max with Bongo after digging up the [[Time Capsule|time capsule]].
