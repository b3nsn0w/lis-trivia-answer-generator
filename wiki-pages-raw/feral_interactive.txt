= Feral Interactive =

[[File:feral-interactive-logo.png|thumb|309x309px]]
'''Feral Interactive''' is an independent, private video-game-porting studio based in London, UK. It was founded in 1996 and works with games publishers and developers to port their PC and console games to other platforms. They serve a global area and currently work with [[Square Enix]] to port their [[Life is Strange (franchise)|''Life is Strange'' franchise]] to Mac and Linux.

A port of [[Life is Strange 2]] is currently listed on their website as "coming soon."<ref>As at August 6th, 2019.</ref>

The studio's mission statement is "to bring great games to the Mac" and also state that they "have established [themselves] as a world-leading publisher of games on Mac and, more recently, Linux."<ref>[https://www.feralinteractive.com/en/about/ Feral Interactive website's "About" page].</ref>

The studio is open to suggestions for any games that people would like to see them port to Mac, Linux or mobile.
