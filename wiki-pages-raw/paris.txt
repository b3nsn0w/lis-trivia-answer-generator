= Paris =

'''Paris''', the capital of France, is a recurring location in the [[Life is Strange (Franchise)|''Life is Strange'' franchise]]. References to this city can be explained by [[Don't Nod|Dontnod Entertainment]]'s studio being based in Paris.

== Mentions ==
=== Life is Strange ===
* In the [[Life is Strange|original game]], [[Maxine Caulfield|Max Caulfield]]'s [[Max's Diary|diary entry]] for August 25, 2013, reads: "So I can cut my hair, get a tat and some piercings... Maybe date a cute foreign exchange artiste from Paris or Rome. I can do anything. Unless I get busted."
* When Max looks at the travel money jar in the [[Madsen Household|Madsen household]]: "Oh, I'd love to go to Paris too. At this rate they'll be lucky to get to [[Portland]]."
* In the [[Alternative Timeline|alternative timeline]] when Max looks at an Eiffel Tower souvenir in the living room: "Bonjour! That's great they finally went to Paris."
* Max asking the alternative timeline [[William Price]] about a trip: "So you finally made it to Paris?"
* When Max returns to the [[Timeline (Life is Strange)|original timeline]] and sees the travel money jar once again: "Looks like theyâ€™re back on the road to Paris."

=== Life is Strange: Before the Storm ===
* In ''[[Life is Strange: Before the Storm|Before the Storm]]'', a photo in [[Rachel Amber (Before the Storm)|Rachel Amber]]'s [[Amber House|house]] shows the Amber family in front of the Eiffel Tower.

=== Life is Strange 2 ===
* [[File:Travel Book Karen's Room.png|thumb|181x181px]] In ''[[Life is Strange 2]]'', the cover of a travel book found in [[Karen Reynolds]]'s [[Karen's Room|room]] depicts the Eiffel Tower.
