'''Kristen''' is one of [[Maxine Caulfield|Max Caulfield]]'s friends in [[Seattle]]. She went to the {{Wiki|Fremont Troll}} with Max and [[Fernando]].

She can first be seen and mentioned in [[Episode 1: Chrysalis|Episode 1]] if Max looks at some photos in her [[Max's Room|room]]. She mentions that Kristen and [[Fernando]] were drunk at the Fremont Troll.
