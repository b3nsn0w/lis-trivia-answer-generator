= Shinobu Matsumoto =

[[File:Shinobu Matsumoto.jpg|thumb]]
'''Shinobu Matsumoto''' ('''まつもと しのぶ''') / ('''松本 忍''') is the Japanese voice actor of William Price ('''ウィリアム・プライス''') for the [[Japanese Release (Life is Strange)|Japanese localization]] of ''[[Life is Strange]]'' and'' [[Life is Strange: Before the Storm]]''.
