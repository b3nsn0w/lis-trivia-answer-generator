= Masahiro Yamanaka =

[[File:Masahiro_Yamanaka.jpg|thumb]]
'''Masahiro Yamanaka''' ('''ヤマナカ マサヒロ''') / ('''山中 真尋''') is the Japanese voice actor of [[Mark Jefferson]] ('''マーク・ジェファソン''') for the [[Japanese Release (Life is Strange)|Japanese localization]] of ''[[Life is Strange]]''.
