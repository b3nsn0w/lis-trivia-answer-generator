= Nathan and Victoria =

[[File:Nathan and Victoria (square).png|right|200px]]
This article is meant to give a deeper and more detailed look at the relationship between [[Nathan Prescott]] and [[Victoria Chase]] and it's development throughout ''[[Life is Strange]]''. They are members of the [[Vortex Club]], in which both of them are considered the co-leaders.

== Background ==
Both Nathan and Victoria come from wealthy families. The [[Prescott Family]] is known to be the oldest and most influential family in [[Arcadia Bay]], in which they paid for a dormitory within [[Blackwell Academy]]. Victoria's Parents own a famous gallery called "Chase Space" which is located in [[Seattle]]. Perhaps because of this, they are best friends, with [[Maxine Caulfield|Max Caulfield]] calling them parallels of each other.
