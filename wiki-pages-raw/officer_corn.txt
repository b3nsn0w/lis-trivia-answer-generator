= Officer Corn =

'''Officer Corn''' is an officer of the local police force, the [[Arcadia Bay Police Department]].

During "[[Episode 3: Chaos Theory|Chaos Theory]]", Officer Corn leaves a message on the answering machine in the [[Madsen Household|Madsen household]] to inform [[David Madsen]] or [[Joyce Price]] that [[Chloe Price]]'s [[Chloe's Truck|pickup truck]] was spotted in the vicinity of [[Blackwell Academy]] near the time of the break-in.
