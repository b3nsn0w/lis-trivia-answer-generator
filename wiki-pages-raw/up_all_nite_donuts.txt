= Up All Nite Donuts =

'''Up All Nite Donuts''' is a location in ''[[Life is Strange]]''. It is a 24-hour food establishment in [[Arcadia Bay]]. This location is not visited at all in the game.
[[File:Chloecomp-police.png|thumb|220x220px|Report about an incident at Up All Nite Donuts on the Police Department's website.]]
It is located off Peckinpah Road and a police incident was reported there at 8:52 PM on October 8th by the [[Arcadia Bay Police Department]]. A nude man with a sword was arrested on the charge of being hopped up on both energy drinks and methamphetamines. He was held without bail pending further investigation.

This small article appears on [[Chloe Price|Chloe]]'s [[Chloe's Computer|laptop]] in [[Episode 3: Chaos Theory|Episode 3]], after the girls make their escape from [[Blackwell Academy|Blackwell]] after breaking into the [[Blackwell Swimming Pool|swimming pool]].
