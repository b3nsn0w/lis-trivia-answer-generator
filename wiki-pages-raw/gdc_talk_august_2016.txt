= GDC Talk August 2016 =

At the '''GDC (Game Development Conference) Europe''' that took place in Cologne in August 2016, Writer [[Christian Divine]] presented a talk titled "The Blue Age of Storytelling" in which he reflects on the writing and design process for ''[[Life is Strange]]'', and how its development heralds a new era for narrative games.
